The Power of a Targeted CV

In today's digital recruitment landscape, a significant hurdle for job seekers is the Applicant Tracking System (ATS). These automated software programs scan and filter resumes based on keywords and criteria derived from the job description. Without proper optimization, even highly qualified candidates can be overlooked. The CV Architect specializes in understanding how ATS works, strategically embedding relevant keywords and formatting your CV to ensure it sails through these initial filters.

Beyond ATS, our tailored approach speaks directly to the needs of hiring managers. We transform your general career history into a compelling narrative that clearly demonstrates how your skills and experiences are the perfect match for the advertised position. This eliminates the need for recruiters to connect the dots, saving them time and immediately positioning you as a top candidate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "standard CV"?

A standard CV is your general resume that you might use for broad applications. It typically lists your work history, education, skills, and achievements without specific tailoring to a single job. We use this as a base to create your targeted CV.

Why do I need to submit the job advertisement?

The job advertisement is crucial as it contains the specific keywords, requirements, and expectations that we will use to customize your CV. Without it, we cannot effectively tailor your document to the target role.

What if the job closing date is less than 48 hours away?

While we strive to assist all clients, our 48-hour lead time is essential to maintain the quality and thoroughness of our customization process. Requests submitted within 48 hours of the closing date may be accepted depending on the volume of the request we have at that particular time.

Can I pay for multiple CV customizations at once?

Yes, you can pay for multiple customizations. Please indicate in your email how many jobs you are requesting tailored CVs for, and adjust your payment accordingly (e.g., R200 for two CVs). Remember to submit each corresponding job advertisement.
